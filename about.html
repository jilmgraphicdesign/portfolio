About Jenel

Biography

Born on October, 19 1988, Jenel was raised in Broward County, South Florida. After highschool she traveled to New York City for the first half of her education and finished school in Las Vegas, Nevada where she lived from 2010-2017.

Current

In 2017 Jenel moved to Charlotte, NC and currently lives there with her husband and son.

Education

  • 2019-2020 Cornell University: Web Design and Development Certificate
  • 2020 Cornell University: Creative Thinking Certificate
